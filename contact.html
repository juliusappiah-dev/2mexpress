Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How can I track my parcel?

You can track your parcel by visiting our Track Parcel page and entering your tracking number. You can also call our customer service line for assistance.

What are your payment options?

We accept cash payments at our terminals, mobile money payments, and bank transfers. We're also working on adding card payment options at our terminals.

How early should I arrive before departure?

We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before your scheduled departure time. This allows enough time for check-in and boarding procedures.

What is your luggage policy?

Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag and two checked bags. Additional bags may be subject to extra fees. Please visit our Terms & Conditions page for detailed information.
