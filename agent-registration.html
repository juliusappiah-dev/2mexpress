Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about becoming a 2M Express agent

What are the requirements to become an agent?

To become a 2M Express agent, you need a physical business location, valid identification, and business registration documents (if applicable). Your location should be easily accessible to customers.

How much commission will I earn?

Commission rates vary based on the services provided and sales volume. Our standard commission structure is competitive within the industry, and we offer bonuses for high-performing agents.

What training will I receive?

We provide comprehensive training on our booking system, ticket issuance, parcel handling procedures, and customer service standards. Training can be conducted at your location or online.

How long does the application process take?

The application process typically takes 5-7 business days. This includes verification of your documents, business location assessment, and system setup.
