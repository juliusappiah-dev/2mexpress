Our Story

Founded in 2021 by our Late Hon. Daniel Okyem Aboagye, 2Mexpress began with a simple mission: to transform the way people travel between cities. What started as a small fleet of vehicles has grown into the region's premier transportation network.

Over the past years, we've invested in state-of-the-art vehicles, innovative booking technology, and rigorous driver training to ensure every journey with us exceeds expectations.
